The Case
The Client

A well-established sofa specialist with a strong physical presence across Barcelona — multiple showrooms, eight years of trading, real product, real customers walking in.

But online? The brand was invisible. An Instagram account that looked abandoned, no consistent posting, no community, no traffic. A great offline business with a digital storefront that was actively losing customers to competitors who knew how to show up.

That's where I came in.

The Plan

Three months.
Three phases.

A clear, sequenced strategy designed for a dormant account: start loud, learn fast, then double down only on what's working.

01
Awareness · High Impact

Intense awareness phase

Concentrated budget over a few high-impact days. Launched 5 varied ad creatives to rapidly test angles and reach new audiences from a cold start.

Goal Build broad exposure fast for a dormant account with no prior activity.
02
Analysis · Selection

Quick analysis & winner selection

Reviewed performance data immediately after the awareness push. Identified the 2 top-performing ads on early engagement and cost-efficiency. Pivoted fast — no wasted spend on underperformers.

Goal Stop guessing. Let the data pick the winners.
03
Retargeting · Conversion

Selective retargeting activation

Re-engaged warm audiences who'd already seen the initial ads. Delivered the highest-converting creatives with clear calls-to-action — link in bio, profile visits, store traffic.

Outcome Amplified interactions, link clicks and profile traffic from familiar faces.
